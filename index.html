How do we do it

  • Adapted program
    preparation for UPE

    You will learn grammar and vocabulary at the same time. And you will learnhow to write essays.

  • Classroom and online learning

    The teacher will help not only in the classroom, but also during home prepartion. Parents can chat if they wish.

  • The habit of reading in English

    Articles from sources that are used in the development of this UPE will be received by phone twice a week.

  • Psychological
    support

    We will teach you testing techniques, the ability to plan your confidence training in itself.

  • Training on copyright manuals

    You don't have to waste your time on a search for material and to buy anything extra. We have compiled 2 manuals for you.

  • Ability to comprehen spoken English

    "F.R.I.E.N.D.S" won't help you. You will listen regularly and analyze audio recordings in accordance with the studied vocabulary.

Teacher

Elena Moreva

  • 6 yeards of experience in preparing students for UPE
  • Own result of passing VNO - 197 points 2 years in a row
  • Passed excellent at the international analogue of UPE - Cambridge PET (Preliminary English Test)

Graduated from the Institute of International Relations of the NAU with a degree in translator-analyst.

In spare time takes courses from the best universities in the world on effective teaching and essay writing techniques.

Completed: English Grammar and Essay Writing (Berkeley), English Grammar & Style (The University of Queesland), How to Write an Essay (Berkeley), Academic and Business Writing (Berkeley), The Art of Teaching (GEMS education), Powerful Tools for Teaching and Learning (University of Houston).

We guarantee

  • You will pass the UPE with at least 25 points better than on the first practice test. Otherwise, we will refund your money.*

  • You will learn to write essays with at least 10 points out of 14. While 20% of the students received 0 for essays.

  • You will study in your area. We will choose the location for the classes after the teams have been formed.

  • Teams will be formed based on the starting level of the students. We take into account the characteristics of each student.
